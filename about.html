About

A small, ongoing project.

Dao De Zion is a small study group and library focused on philosophy, scripture, and first-person inquiry.

It exists alongside Christopher Shenefelt’s personal archive, but is more focused and selective. The aim is to gather a small number of serious texts and to read them carefully over time.

Founder

Dao De Zion was founded by Christopher Shenefelt as a simple place for study, discussion, and the slow formation of a shared reading life.
