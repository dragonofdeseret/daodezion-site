About

A living institution in first form

Dao De Zion is an attempt to convene philosophy, scripture, and first-person inquiry within one disciplined frame. Its initial form is intentionally spare: enough to establish tone, canon, and direction, while leaving room for a deeper archive, a lecture body, and a study fellowship to emerge.

This site stands alongside Christopher Shenefelt’s personal archive, but is not identical with it. The personal site holds the broader body of work. Dao De Zion is the more curated, institutional, and future-facing expression.
