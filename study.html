Study group

A simple format for shared inquiry

The study program should be disciplined without becoming rigid: clear enough to guide a circle, open enough to allow revelation, insight, and disagreement.

  • Opening5 minutes of silence and a short statement of intention.
  • Primary textRead one short passage from Dao, De, or Zion aloud.
  • RoundtableEach person names one line, image, or tension that stood out.
  • SynthesisAsk how the passage reshapes perception, conduct, or community.
  • CloseName the next reading and one practical question to carry forward.
