The Witness

This path concerns lived experience and first-person view: what remains when inherited forms loosen, what is disclosed inwardly, and how consciousness changes when it is no longer forced to speak only in borrowed terms.

The witness is not private indulgence. It is the discipline of learning what one actually encounters, suffers, intuits, symbolizes, and sees before those things are reduced to doctrinal shorthand.
