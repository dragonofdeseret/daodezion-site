Library

A small working canon.

The library is organized into three paths: the Way, the Witness, and the Order. Each begins with a small set of core texts, followed by further reading.

I

The Way (Dao)

  • Core Daodejing · Shōbōgenzō · Bhagavad Gita
  • Further Heart Sutra · Diamond Sutra · Platform Sutra · The Gateless Gate
II

The Witness (De)

  • Core On Having No Head · The Science of the First Person · The Prophet
  • Further Alan Watts · The Red Book · Man and His Symbols · Iron John · Neuroscience of Personality
III

The Order (Zion)

  • Core Book of Mormon · Torah · Analects
  • Further Pearl of Great Price · Jesus the Christ · Dhammapada · Sri Chaitanya & His Associates

This library is intentionally small. It is meant to give the group a stable starting place rather than an overwhelming system.
