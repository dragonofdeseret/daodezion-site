How the circle should proceed

The aim is neither loose conversation nor rigid scholasticism, but disciplined openness.

  • Read slowlyGive precedence to the text before opinion.
  • Speak concretelyName passages, images, and exact tensions.
  • Permit silenceNot every gap must be filled.
  • Compare carefullyCross-tradition reading should clarify, not flatten difference.
  • Close with practiceEach meeting should end with one lived question.
