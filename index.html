For the study, production, and transmission of enduring ideas.

Dao De Zion is an emerging intellectual institution organized around three paths: Dao, De, and Zion. It joins Eastern philosophy, personal revelation, and theological inquiry in a space meant to be read slowly, studied together, and expanded over time.

Core canon

Three chambers of study

The institution is arranged as a triadic library: Dao for metaphysical orientation, De for first-person realization, and Zion for covenant, order, and communal life.

Study group

A simple format for tonight

  • Opening5 minutes of silence and a short statement of intention.
  • Primary textRead one short passage from Dao, De, or Zion aloud.
  • RoundtableEach person names one line, image, or tension that stood out.
  • SynthesisAsk how the passage reshapes perception, conduct, or community.
  • CloseName the next reading and one practical question to carry forward.

About

A living institution in first form

This first version is intentionally spare. Its purpose is to establish tone, canon, and direction: enough to convene a serious reading circle now, while leaving room for a fuller archive, lecture series, and collaborative body of work later.
