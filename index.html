Dao · De · Zion

A small circle for serious reading.

Dao De Zion is a simple study group and library organized around three paths: the Way (Dao), the Witness (De), and the Order (Zion).

We meet monthly to read, discuss, and think together. The aim is not volume, but depth — to engage a small number of texts carefully, and to let them shape perception, conduct, and community over time.
