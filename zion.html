The Order

This path concerns sacred form: covenant, law, memory, lineage, ethical pattern, and the attempt to make a durable people rather than a merely inspired mood.

Order is often what readers leave before they can recognize what in it was still necessary. The problem is not form itself, but form severed from alignment and witness.
